# Output Modes

Choose the output mode that best fits the decision need.

## Executive summary

Use when the reader needs:
- the answer fast
- implications
- priorities
- recommendation

Best for:
- leadership updates
- quick stakeholder reads
- short user-facing conclusions

## Technical memo

Use when the reader needs:
- detail
- methodology
- caveats
- traceability

Best for:
- engineering analysis
- tooling evaluation
- architecture or operations notes

## Applied recommendation

Use when the reader needs:
- a concrete adaptation to a real environment
- prioritization
- what to build or change first

Best for:
- strategy into action
- tool adoption plans
- Lince/OpenClaw recommendations

## Decision brief

Use when the reader needs:
- options
- tradeoffs
- recommendation
- next step

Best for:
- choosing between approaches
- deciding whether to adopt, defer, or ignore something
